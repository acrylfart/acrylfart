About the Artist

acrylfart is the artistic identity of Matus Brun, a contemporary painter based in Slovakia. His work explores the boundaries between abstraction and figurative representation, creating unique visual experiences that engage the viewer on multiple levels.

With a background in graphic design and a passion for color theory, Matus develops complex compositions that balance spontaneity with meticulous planning. His creative process often begins with intuitive mark-making, followed by layers of refinement that bring depth and texture to each piece.

acrylfart's work has been exhibited in galleries throughout Europe and is held in private collections internationally. He continues to push the boundaries of acrylic painting, experimenting with new techniques and approaches to create compelling visual narratives.
